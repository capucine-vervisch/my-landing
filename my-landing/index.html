Biography

Fred Vervisch is an artist born in 1970, in France. He worked, first of all, for the Walt Disney Company as a storyboarder. Now, he has two jobs: storyboarder and comic-strip's drawer. In his free time, he also enjoys drawing. He has his own style, very diversified. So let's discover some of these
